FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about solar energy and our services.

What size solar system do I need?

The size depends on your energy consumption, roof space and budget. We conduct a thorough assessment and recommend a system tailored to your needs.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations are completed within 1–3 days depending on system size and roof complexity. Commercial projects may take longer.

Can solar power my entire home?

Yes. With the right system design and sufficient battery storage, a solar system can power your entire home including appliances, lights and electronics.

Do you offer maintenance?

Yes. We provide routine maintenance services to keep your system performing at its best, including panel cleaning, inverter checks and battery health monitoring.

How long do solar batteries last?

High-quality solar batteries typically last 5–15 years depending on usage, temperature and maintenance. We recommend the best options for your needs.
