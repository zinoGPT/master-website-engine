BUILD RULES
====================================================

Never redesign the layout.

Always keep the section order.

Always use local images.

Never invent company history.

Never invent customer testimonials.

Never invent awards.

Never invent certifications.

Never invent years of experience.

Never invent project statistics.

If company information is missing, simply omit it while preserving the layout.

Always keep the design responsive.

Always optimise for mobile devices.

Prioritise readability over decoration.

Every section should encourage trust.

The website should look premium enough that the business owner immediately wants it.
